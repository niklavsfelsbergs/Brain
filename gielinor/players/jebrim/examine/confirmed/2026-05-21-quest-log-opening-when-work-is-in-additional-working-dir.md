# Quest-log present whenever work crystallizes — not just turn 1

## Before / after

**Before ([[S024_2026-05-21_shipping-agent-rulebook-revamp|S024]]):** session opened with "hey jebrim," ran 15 turns editing shipping-agent/ in the additional working dir, zero quest-log activity. Principal asked at T11 "do you have any quests open?" — 15 substantive turns off-record. A crash would have left nothing recoverable.

**After:** turn 1 reads `quest-log/in-progress/` to detect resume vs fresh. **Resume → append on turn 1.** **Fresh → open the moment substantive work lands** (first non-trivial edit, query, or artifact), not at turn N. Close-session renames new ones to SNNN.

## The rule

The trigger isn't "Jebrim activated" — it's "substantive work is about to land or has just landed." Where it lands (gielinor or an additional working dir) is irrelevant. One-shot read-only Q&A doesn't earn a stub.

When in doubt, open the entry. A wasted stub is one file; a missed log is a whole session falling off the record.
