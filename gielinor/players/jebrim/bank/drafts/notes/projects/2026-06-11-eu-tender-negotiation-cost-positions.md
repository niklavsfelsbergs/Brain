## The framing (the ask Niklavs wanted)

"We'll award you more volume; to make it a good deal, drop/cap this one cost position." Works best when the position is ancillary (not base rates), small vs the carrier's revenue gain, and benchmarkable against a competitor that doesn't charge it.
