## §U — Shared / persistent-attach PTY (parked 2026-05-31, [[S137_5b18b6f6_cockpit-phone-access-and-handoff|S137]])

**The gap it closes.** Today `ptybridge.py` is one-WS⇄one-PTY⇄one-claude, and the PTY is `proc.terminate`'d the moment its WebSocket closes. This *supersedes* the [[S137_5b18b6f6_cockpit-phone-access-and-handoff|S137]] handoff (kill-then-resume becomes unnecessary for same-conversation takeover) — keep handoff only as the cross-machine "move the process" case if still wanted.
