# Every money figure states the period it covers

**2026-05-23.** In the quota-reduction deck I labelled the 5-pp target as "€4.4M / year savings needed." €4.4M was the **6-month** figure; annualized it is €8.4M. A 2× error on a headline KPI built for a commercial audience, caught only on my own re-read — not before it went into the bundle.

**Rule.** Every money figure carries the period it covers, stated explicitly. When annualizing, do it openly (×2 for a 6-month sum) and never let a period-scoped sum wear a "/year" label. Cross-check headline KPIs against the window of the data they came from *before* they land in a deliverable — a period/scale slip on a headline number is a credibility hit, not a footnote.
