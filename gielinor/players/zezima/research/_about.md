# research/ — full research writeups (Zezima)

**Cognitive role.** Source material — full writeups from research passes, anchored to URLs and primary sources. The research layer is *where the work shows*; the bank layer is where the *takeaways* live.

## Who writes here

- **Penguins** (the research sub-agent) write freely — this is their playground. No draft gate inside this folder.
- **Principals** running the research skill personally write here too.

## What goes here

Research writeups in the shape prescribed by `gielinor/spellbook/skills/research.md`:

- **Question** — what was being asked.
- **Date of research** — `YYYY-MM-DD`; sources age fast.
- **Confidence** — high / medium / low based on source quality and gap size.
- **Sources** — bulleted list with title, URL, and *what this is, why it's load-bearing*.
- **Findings** — prose, bullets, or sections as the material warrants. Inline citations and inference flags.
- **Gaps & open questions** — what couldn't be confirmed; known-unknowns.

## Filenames

`YYYY-MM-DD-<topic-slug>.md`. Date is when the research was done.

## What does not go here

Distilled / picked-out claims belong in `bank/drafts/notes/` (then promoted to `bank/notes/`). The research file is the source; the bank note is the picking. See `gielinor/meta/layer-routing.md` for the picking flow.

## Promotion

None — research files stay in `research/` as the anchor for whatever bank notes derive from them. When a writeup is superseded, the old file moves to `research/archive/` per `gielinor/meta/archive-discipline.md`. The picked bank notes that depended on it follow the same archive flow.
