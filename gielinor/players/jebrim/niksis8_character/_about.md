# Jebrim — niksis8_character/

**Cognitive role.** What Jebrim knows about Niklavs through *his* relationship — the per-player lens on the user.

**Scope.** Per-player. Universal facts live in `gielinor/niksis8/`.

## What goes here

- Things about Niklavs that show up *in the work register* — how he frames a task, what he wants in a deliverable, how he expects to be challenged.
- Stakeholder context: who Niklavs is working with at any given time, what their needs are, how Niklavs prefers to mediate between them and the work.
- Communication preferences in the work register specifically — which may differ from his preferences with Zezima.

## What does not go here

- Facts about Niklavs that hold regardless of player — `gielinor/niksis8/`.
- Observations about Jebrim himself — `examine/`.
- Today's work state — `inventory/` or `bank/`.
