- **Change 6 — Understanding/Plan preamble protocol.** Every response opens with two short lines (bold labels, single sentence each): `**Understanding:** ...` restating the ask in the agent's own words, `**Plan:** ...` restating the intended action, then the substantive response. Compress to one action line for trivial asks. Applies in every mode (player, unscoped, alching, bankstanding) and every role (principal, dwarf); voice adapts to the active character, structure does not. Internal rituals (respawn, mini-respawn, threshold checks) stay silent — but a threshold *recommendation* surfaces as one line after the plan. Why: misunderstanding catch (a one-line restatement is cheap to correct) and transparency of intent. Created `gielinor/meta/communication-protocol.md` with the full rule + rationale; added it to the `@import` block in master `CLAUDE.md`. Added a prominent "Communication protocol — read first" section near the top of master `CLAUDE.md` (before "What you are") with the compact format and a pointer to the full rule.
