Trees: dropped the four edge-march loops; replaced with `TREE_CANDIDATES = 130` (was 520, dialed 4× down on user note) jittered scatter with acceptance probability inversely weighted by distance-to-edge — dense perimeter, thin middle, no rectangular hedge. Buildings: new `wallTexture(w, d, h, type)` for stone-course or wood-plank seams on SW (shadow) + SE (lit) walls with faint highlight lines above each course; new `roofTexture()` for shingle rows. `isoBuilding` extended with `texture: 'stone'|'wood'` and `roofTex: true`; tagged all 9 buildings (stone: Meta Town Hall, Lorebook, Bank, Hall of Mirrors, Spellbook Tower, Keepsake Vault; wood: Quest Hall, The Inn, The Workshop).
