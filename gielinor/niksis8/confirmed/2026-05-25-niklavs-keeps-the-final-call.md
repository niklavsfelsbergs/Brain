# Niklavs keeps the final call for himself — analysis is decision-support he adjudicates, never the verdict

**Cross-player pattern ([[B-007_2026-05-25_seventh-bankstanding|B-007]] synthesis, first N=2 cross-read).** The same trait is confirmed independently in two domains, through two players' lenses: Niklavs treats analytical output — an agent's finding, a research synthesis — as *input he adjudicates*, not as the verdict.

- **Work lens — Jebrim `niklavs-verifies-agent-claims-by-cross-checking` ([[S023_2026-05-21_shipping-mart-coverage-audit|S023]]).** When the shipping-agent surfaced a concerning coverage claim, he didn't trust it and didn't ask it to defend itself — he commissioned an *independent* second read from a different agent, then adjudicated (numbers confirmed, framing corrected). He won't take a single source as truth.
- **Personal lens — Zezima `home-decisions-gut-fit-veto` ([[S056_e5df54a2_apartment-domajamas-dalas-research|S056]]) + `space-is-the-binding-home-buy-constraint` ([[S066_e2362ea0_domajamas-dalas-reconsider|S066]]).** Research's job was to *rule out*, not to talk him in; the final call was his felt gut-fit, which overrode clean legal/financial analysis on two consecutive flats. He passes cleanly when the fit is wrong, even against favourable data.

**The unified trait.** He does not outsource the verdict. In work it shows as distrust-of-single-source (cross-check, adjudicate); in personal life as felt-judgment-overrides-data (gut-fit veto). Both: the analysis informs, he decides.

**How to apply (any player).** Lead with decision-support, not conclusions. Work: name the next probe and flag a finding's own breakage points; expect cross-checks; frame corrections diagnostically, not dismissively. Personal: surface dealbreakers and trade-offs, trust his veto, don't push "the numbers say X." Give him grounds to decide; don't decide for him.

**Scope note.** This is the *adjudicator* trait, domain-general. Distinct from Jebrim's `pride-tied-to-execution-under-resource-friction`, which is self-flagged work-shaped and explicitly NOT graduated.
