# `player/` — about

User-model for dev sessions. What I notice and track about Niklavs — preferences, working agreements, the contract between us.

Metaphor: character info — the principal I work with.

## What goes here

- `preferences.md` — Niklavs's coding, naming, and workflow preferences for this project. Mostly sourced from `~/.claude/CLAUDE.md` (global user preferences); local additions or refinements layer in.
- `working-agreements.md` — explicit collaboration rules co-designed during dev sessions. Currently a stub; the rules-shaped piece may be subsumed by `examine/identity.md` postures or may stand alongside. See [[plan]] §H.4.

## Distinct from `examine/`

`examine/` is *me looking at myself*. `player/` is *me looking at the principal*. They're complementary halves of the working dyad. An identity ruling like "match register to principal's signals" lives in `examine/` (it's about my posture); a fact like "Niklavs prefers polars over pandas" lives in `player/` (it's about him).

## Conventions

- Small. This isn't a research dossier on the user — it's working knowledge.
- Source preferences from `~/.claude/CLAUDE.md` rather than re-stating; this folder *points*, with local additions only.
- Update preferences when Niklavs corrects me on something that's a stable preference (not a one-off).
