# S008 — 2026-05-21 — Iso visualizer v0 ([[Q-007_gielinor_visualizer|Q-007]] replay-from-git-log)

Same calendar day as [[S007_bankstanding_phase_0_alching]]. Niklavs asked for a one-shot of the [[Q-007]] visualizer — explicitly "I just want to see how it would look." The trigger gate ("real dwarf wave runs *and* first bankstanding produces real motion to render") was partially met when Jebrim's [[S002_dev_brain_runescape_restructure|S002]] wave completed earlier in the day; replay-from-git-log v0 was unblocked regardless because commits suffice. No `D-NNN` had been drafted at the start — the v0 got built, then formalized as [[D-008_iso_replay_v0_over_three_js]] retroactively.

- **Built `developer-braindead/experiments/visualizer/index.html`** — single self-contained HTML file, ~1700 lines. Top-down v0 first, then rebuilt as isometric after Niklavs pushed for RuneScape aesthetics. Engine survives both renderings unchanged: master `EVENTS` array timestamped from real commits [[S001_dev_brain_architecture|S001]]–[[S007_bankstanding_phase_0_alching|S007]] + [[Q-007_gielinor_visualizer|Q-007]] open, `applyEvent` dispatcher with `instant` flag for scrub-seeking, CSS-transitions for movement, RAF tick loop.
- **Iso vs three.js — [[D-008_iso_replay_v0_over_three_js]] codifies it.** Niklavs proposed 3D ("That would mean 3d"). I pushed back: real 3D in one shot looks worse than handcrafted iso because RuneScape's look comes from artistic asset work, not geometry. We went iso. Decision documented separately.
- **Five visible-iteration cycles after the iso landed.** (1) SVG `<symbol>` defaulting to 100% of viewport — trees and players rendered enormous; fixed by converting symbols to `<g>` defs. (2) Grass diamond mis-centered with `ISO.offY=80` putting it at the top of the screen — half the buildings floated in void; fixed by moving to `offY=440` and switching mask shape. (3) Hall of Mirrors dome was double the wall-base diameter — looked like a UFO; shrunk to match. (4) Trees outside the diamond were "floating in void"; rewrote `buildFlora()` to compute positions from iso `(cx, cy)` coords with building-collision check. (5) Tree sway animation rotated around SVG viewport origin instead of tree base (missing `transform-box: fill-box`) — trees appeared to walk across the map; animation killed. (6) Grass diamond clipped at corners leaving black wedges; switched from diamond mask to viewport-crop with radial vignette.
- **What the artifact renders.** 9 buildings as iso sprites with per-building character (Bank coin pile, Lorebook stacked books on roof slope, Spellbook Tower's pulsing aura, Inn chimney smoke, Keepsake Vault's glowing gem, Hall of Mirrors dome, Quest Hall timber framing and torches, Meta Town Hall spire and banners, Inbox Square stone tiles + bulletin board). Cobblestone paths between buildings drawn as iso-diamond stones. Pond with ripples and lily pad. Trees + bushes + flowers + rocks scattered on grass. Compass rose. Footstep dust during walks. Active-player glow. Wisp glow filter for unscoped sessions. Pyramidal building roofs with two visible slopes, stone plinths, three-layer shadows.

**Cascade.** Dev-brain files: this quest-log entry; `bank/decisions/D-008_iso_replay_v0_over_three_js.md`; `bank/open-questions/Q-007_gielinor_visualizer.md` updated (status `open` → `working — v0 shipped`).

**Discipline observation (candidate [[I-002_render_before_shipping_ui|I-002]]).** Each visual issue Niklavs flagged was something a single screenshot would have caught before shipping: the `<symbol>` sizing was obvious in the rendered page, the grass-offset showed half-empty void, the floating trees were in the corners. The pattern: I committed to a one-shot, the model "compiled" in my head but I never *rendered* it. Worth promoting to `examine/I-NNN` at session close — *when generating UI, predict the visual by mentally rendering the SVG/CSS, not by checking the code is valid*. Four iterations of pushback for visible bugs is the threshold.
