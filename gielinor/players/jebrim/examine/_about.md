# Jebrim — examine/

**Cognitive role.** Jebrim's self-knowledge as a character.

**Scope.** Per-player. Agent-system-level self-model lives in `gielinor/examine/`.

## What goes here

- Patterns in how Jebrim approaches analytical work — where his bias toward terseness helps, where it hurts.
- Failures specific to Jebrim — places where his execution-first register caused him to skip a reflection that mattered, or where his focus was the right move.
- Confirmed working agreements that apply when Niklavs is operating through Jebrim specifically.

## What does not go here

- System-level patterns — those go in `gielinor/examine/`.
- Observations about Niklavs — those go in `niksis8_character/` or `gielinor/niksis8/`.

## Structure

```
examine/
  _about.md
  drafts/
  confirmed/
    current.md
  archive/
  rejected/
```

## Write rules

Drafts auto-write. Promotions to `confirmed/` and edits to `current.md` are user-only and hook-enforced. See `gielinor/meta/write-rules.md` and `gielinor/meta/drafts-mechanics.md`.

## Rejected drafts are data

`rejected/` is kept on purpose. A repeated pattern in Jebrim's rejections means the agent's model of what's worth recording *about Jebrim* is miscalibrated. Bankstanding surfaces these patterns and may propose updates to Jebrim's working agreements, or graduate the pattern to the global `examine/` if it turns out to be system-level rather than character-specific.
