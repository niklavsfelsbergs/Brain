## Done — one-line synthesis
Practitioner consensus (state portal + law firms + named advokāts Egīls Radziņš) is to avoid buying a domājamā daļa if possible; the practical risks are uncooperative co-owners blocking decisions, a single co-owner forcing a below-market auction of the WHOLE building, a co-owner's creditors auctioning their share (no pre-emption right at forced auction → stranger co-owner), and cost-sharing friction. Most load-bearing pre-purchase check: a written AND Zemesgrāmata-registered lietošanas kārtības līgums securing access to YOUR apartment. Recent shift: Senāts SKC-108/2025 — usage-utilities = the user pays; management + property tax = all co-owners by share.
