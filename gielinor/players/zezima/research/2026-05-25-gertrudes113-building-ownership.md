# Ģertrūdes iela 113, Rīga — the building, its ownership, and the 6th-floor addition

**Subject.** The specific apartment for sale at Ģertrūdes iela 113, Rīga (ss.lv listing
`befhkm`): ~58 m², 2 isolated rooms, floor 5/6, pre-war brick, individual gas heat, asking
€120,000 (~€2,069/m²), held as *domājamās daļas* (ideal co-ownership shares), "Zeme īpašumā."

**Brief.** Building- and ownership-structure focus: apartment/co-owner count; the 6th-floor +
roof addition (when, by whom, legal status, effect on the share split); construction &
condition; ownership form & management; public records.

**Date of research.** 2026-05-25.
**Researcher.** Penguin (external web research, Zezima namespace).

**Confidence summary.** *High* on: ownership form (kopīpašums / domājamās daļas — confirmed
verbatim from the Land Register folio purpose text via the management association's registered
mērķis), the building being two physical buildings on one folio, the 6th-floor addition's
existence, the 1913 build year and brick construction, the existence and recent (Feb 2025)
registration of a management *biedrība*, and the general law that adding floor area dilutes
existing shares. *Medium* on: the exact apartment count (sources say ~81 *listed/known* units,
which corrects the owner's ~70 upward but is a portal aggregate, not a cadastral count). *Low /
gap*: who built the addition, exactly when, its permit status, and whether the existing co-owners'
domājamās daļas were in fact recalculated after it (needs an authenticated Zemesgrāmata folio
Nr. 5262 pull + a BIS būvniecība lookup — see Gaps).

## 0. The single most important finding (read this first)

**The "biedrība manages it" line in the listing does NOT contradict the domājamās-daļas claim —
it confirms it.** The management association registered for this building states its purpose, in
the Land Register's own language, as uniting the *dzīvokļu īpašnieki* for the management of the
**kopīpašuma esošās domājamās daļas** (the co-ownership's notional shares) of a single
**Dzīvojamā māja** registered in **one Zemesgrāmata folio (nodalījums Nr. 5262)**. A building
that had been split into proper *dzīvokļa īpašumi* would have *separate* folios per flat and the
association would manage *units*, not *shares of a co-ownership*. So this is a **kopīpašnieku /
co-owners' management biedrība**, not a unit-owners' (*dzīvokļu īpašnieku*) biedrība in the
"building is already divided" sense. The naming is the same ("biedrība"); the legal substance is
co-ownership. **The domājamās-daļas structure is confirmed, high confidence.**
Source — verbatim mērķis text (quoted in §4).

### Did the addition change the domājamās daļas? (the core risk — high on the law, gap on this building)
This is the load-bearing legal point for an existing/prospective share-holder, and the law is clear:

- **Adding floor area recalculates everyone's shares.** The State Land Service's own service
  description for *"Atzinums par iesakāmo domājamo daļu aprēķinu"* states that notional shares are
  computed from **floor area**, and — quoting the service page —
  > if data updates to the building change its floor area **(for example, attic construction)**,
  > the recommended shares **will differ** from the shares calculated earlier.
  i.e. when total building area grows, **every existing owner's proportional share is diluted** to
  make room for the new units' shares.
  Source: https://www.vzd.gov.lv/lv/pakalpojumi/apidda
- **The addition required ALL co-owners' consent.** Attic/roof space is *common property*; under
  Civillikums **CL 1068** *"rīkoties ar kopīpašuma priekšmetu ... drīkst tikai ar visu kopīpašnieku
  piekrišanu"* and *"neviens atsevišķs kopīpašnieks nevar bez visu pārējo piekrišanas ... to
  pārgrozīt."* Building new flats in the roof of a kopīpašums **without unanimous co-owner consent
  is invalid and exposes the builder to a damages claim.**
  Source (LV portāls e-consultation, *"Bez pārējo īpašnieku atļaujas bēniņus pārbūvēt nedrīkst"*):
  https://lvportals.lv/e-konsultacijas/13983-bez-parejo-ipasnieku-atlaujas-beninus-parbuvet-nedrikst-2018
- **What this means for the buyer (synthesis / inference):**
  1. If the addition was done **properly**, there exists a unanimous co-owners' agreement and a
     **recalculated domājamo daļu split** that already reflects the new units. The subject flat's
     share is then whatever the post-addition split assigns it — and the new-floor owners hold
     their own shares. *This is the benign case.*
  2. If the addition was done **without full consent or without recalculating shares**, there is a
     latent dispute: a *patvaļīga būvniecība* (unauthorised-construction) exposure and/or an
     un-reconciled share table. *This is the case that should worry a buyer.*
  3. Either way, **the buyer must verify, in folio Nr. 5262, (a) that the new floor is recorded,
     (b) what each existing share now is, and (c) that the consent + permit exist.** A pre-addition
     share that was never recalculated is a red flag.
  *Confidence: high that this is the right question; low on which case applies here — that needs
  the folio + BIS pull (§6).*
- General context on the phenomenon (legal/practical): unauthorised construction in co-ownerships —
  https://lvportals.lv/e-konsultacijas/2063-par-patvaligu-buvniecibu-2013 ; share-change on
  area-change under the Dzīvokļa īpašuma likums —
  https://lvportals.lv/skaidrojumi/284286-jaunumi-dzivoklu-ipasnieku-tiesibas-un-pienakumos-i-2017 ;
  Dzīvokļa īpašuma likums itself — https://m.likumi.lv/ta/id/221382-dzivokla-ipasuma-likums .

**Finding: confirmed kopīpašums / domājamās daļas, one Land Register folio, managed by a
co-owners' biedrība registered 05.02.2025. Confidence: high.**

- **Management body: biedrība "Ģertrūdes 113", reg. Nr. 40008344006**, registered
  **05.02.2025**, **5 board members** (natural persons, names not public on the aggregator),
  registered address **Ģertrūdes iela 113A – 2, Rīga, LV-1009**. Currently active, no debts/
  restrictions noted.
  Sources: https://www.firmas.lv/lv/uznemumi/gertrudes-113/40008344006 ;
  https://company.lursoft.lv/gertrudes-113/40008344006
- **Verbatim purpose (mērķis) — the decisive ownership-form evidence:**
  > "Biedrības mērķis ir apvienot nekustamā īpašuma, ierakstīta Rīgas pilsētas zemesgrāmatas
  > **nodalījumā Nr. 5262** ar kadastra Nr. 0100 032 0073, kas sastāv no zemes vienības **3237
  > kv.m.** platībā ar kadastra apzīmējumu 0100 032 0073 un uz tās esošās **dzīvojamās mājas ar
  > kadastra apzīmējumu 0100 032 0073 001 un dzīvojamās mājas ar kadastra apzīmējumu 0100 032 0073
  > 002**, turpmāk tekstā viss nekustamais īpašums kopā saukts – Dzīvojamā māja, dzīvokļu īpašniekus
  > savas dzīvokļa īpašumā ietilpstošās **Dzīvojamās mājas kopīpašuma esošās domājamās daļas
  > apsaimniekošanai un pārvaldīšanai**."
  Source: https://www.firmas.lv/lv/uznemumi/gertrudes-113/40008344006
- **Reading of the mērķis:**
  - **One folio (nodalījums Nr. 5262)** for the whole property → the building is **NOT split into
    separate dzīvokļa-īpašumu folios**. This is the structural signature of *kopīpašums*. **Confirms
    the domājamās-daļas claim.**
  - **"kopīpašuma esošās domājamās daļas ... pārvaldīšanai"** → the association exists to manage the
    *co-ownership's notional shares*, i.e. it is a **co-owners' management biedrība**. It is **not**
    evidence that the building is divided into units. (This is the contradiction the brief asked me
    to watch for — and it resolves the *other* way: no contradiction.)
  - **Two buildings (073 001 + 073 002) on a 3237 m² parcel**, both inside the same co-ownership.
  - The slightly awkward phrase *"dzīvokļu īpašniekus savas dzīvokļa īpašumā ietilpstošās ...
    domājamās daļas"* mixes "dzīvokļa īpašums" wording with "domājamās daļas" — common drafting in
    co-ownerships that *use* a flat-by-flat usage arrangement but are **legally still kopīpašums**.
    The operative noun is **domājamās daļas of a kopīpašums**, not registered dzīvokļa īpašumi.
    *Confidence: high on the legal substance; the drafting ambiguity is noted, not load-bearing.*
- **Why a Feb-2025 association matters.** It is **new** — under four months old at time of this
  research. A freshly-formed co-owners' biedrative + a recent 6th-floor addition together suggest a
  building whose governance and (possibly) its share table were **recently reorganised**. Whether
  that reorganisation *included* a clean domājamo daļu recalculation post-addition is exactly the
  open question in §2/§6. *Inference.*
- **No older namu pārvalde / hired pārvaldnieks** surfaced for this address (no Rīgas namu
  pārvaldnieks page, no third-party manager). The biedrība appears to be the current manager.
  *Confidence: medium (absence of evidence).*

## 6. Gaps — only closeable by Niklavs (authenticated / official pulls)

1. **Zemesgrāmata folio Nr. 5262 (authenticated pull).** The decisive document. It shows: the full
   **list of co-owners and each one's domājamā daļa** (→ real co-owner count, and whether shares
   were recalculated after the addition); **whether the 6th floor is recorded**; **the registered
   *lietošanas kārtība*** (does the seller's flat have a registered, successor-binding right to
   occupy?); and **encumbrances** (mortgages, aresti, servitudes). Pull via zemesgramata.lv with
   eID / a notary / sworn surveyor. *This single pull closes most of the high-value gaps.*
2. **Exact apartment & co-owner count.** Cadastre telpu-grupu list for buildings 073 001 and
   073 002 (kadastrs.lv authenticated) → the true unit count and which building the ~70 vs ~81
   discrepancy belongs to.
3. **The addition's permit & consent file.** BIS (bis.gov.lv) + Rīga būvvalde: the *būvatļauja*
   for the 6th-floor/roof izbūve, the **unanimous co-owners' consent** that CL 1068 requires, and
   the **act of acceptance into service (akts par pieņemšanu ekspluatācijā)**. Confirms the
   addition is *legal* (not *patvaļīga būvniecība*) and dated.
4. **Whether domājamās daļas were recalculated post-addition.** Cross-check the folio share table
   against the pre-addition state and/or a VZD *"atzinums par iesakāmo domājamo daļu aprēķinu."*
   The law says they *should* change (§2); confirm they *did*.
5. **The biedrība's statūti and the co-owners' agreement.** What the Feb-2025 biedrība actually
   governs, voting rules, the apsaimniekošanas arrangement, any sinking-fund / debts apportioned by
   share. (Statūti via Uzņēmumu reģistra dokumenti; board-member names via UR.)
6. **RVC AZ boundary check.** Confirm whether 113 is inside the strict historic-centre protection
   zone (affects what the roof addition was allowed to be and any ongoing façade obligations).
7. **Building-wide condition/renovation & energy class.** No public energy certificate or
   renovation record found; ask the biedrība / seller for the building's energoefektivitātes
   sertifikāts and any façade/communications renovation history (the 57%-depreciation old body is
   the part that carries future cost).
