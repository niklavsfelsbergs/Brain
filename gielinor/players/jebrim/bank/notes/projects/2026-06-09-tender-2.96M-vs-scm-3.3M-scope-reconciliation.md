# EU-tender €2.96M "today" vs SCM €3.3M — why they differ (population/basis, not error)

**Drafted 2026-06-09 (Jebrim, S187).** Verified live via shipping-agent. Links: [[eu_tender_2026]], [[scm]], [[2026-06-09-routing-cost-basis-decisions]]. Reconciliation detail in completed quest `S187_85b0fcc3_scm-vs-tender-q1-reconciliation`.

**The question that recurs:** the EU-tender deck/decision-report states **€2.96M** Q1 "today" spend (whole 18-country book), but SCM shows **~€3.3M for just 7 countries** (DE,FR,AT,NL,IT,ES,CH). Larger number, fewer countries — the signature of a population/basis widening, not dropped/double-counted data.

**How €2.96M is built** (`2_analysis/sql/population_2026q1.sql`): `production_site = 'PCS PL'` (Stettin print site only) · `shop_order_created_date` in [2026-01-01, 2026-04-01) · weight/L/W/H NOT NULL · `fact_shipment_cost_summary.total_eur IS NOT NULL` (**invoiced-only**) · packagetype NOT NULL · 18-country whitelist · basis = `real_shipping_cost_eur` (freight only; tax + customs excluded). Gross pull ≈ €3.03M / 531,194 parcels, then **UPS OML net-out** (>€400 full-refund, ~69 parcels / ~€75k) → **€2,955,020**.

**Why SCM is bigger** — relaxation ladder, 7 countries:
- L0 tender-exact/7c: €2.80M
- **L1 drop `PCS PL` (all production sites): €3.24M — +€435k, almost all the Wolfen photo-lab site.** ← THE driver.
- L2 final basis (COALESCE real/expected/avg) + drop invoiced-only: €3.61M (+€375k expected tail).
- L3 drop dim/packagetype gates: €4.07M.

**SCM does NOT filter to PCS PL** — it shows all production sites on its default `cost_for_routing = COALESCE(shipping_cost_final, expected)` basis, so it sweeps in the Wolfen photo-lab parcels (and the non-invoiced tail at estimate) that the tender deliberately excludes. `production_site` × `source_system` are crossing axes (Wolfen *site* also prints B2C/Picturator volume), so the origin filter excludes more than one vertical.

**Takeaways:**
- €2.96M is the *correct* scope for the tender (it's the TCG-Picanova / PCS-PL dispatch book) — not comparable to an all-sites SCM view. Reconcile **definition before numbers** when two same-labeled totals disagree.
- **Driver #1 of any tender-vs-SCM gap = `production_site = 'PCS PL'`** (origin restriction), basis (invoiced-only vs COALESCE-expected) second.
- The tender "today" figure is a **point-in-time snapshot of a backfilling number**: live mart had crept ~+2.3% (to ~€3.03M net) within ~hours of the deck build, as late carrier invoices land. Quote it with an "as of" date.
