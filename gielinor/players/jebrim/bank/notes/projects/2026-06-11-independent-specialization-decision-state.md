- The seam is open because: US contingency SaaS is FedEx/UPS-centric with shallow EU coverage; enterprise FAP/advisory is freight-first and retainer-priced; big firms benchmark at category grain; EU platforms compare freight bids but do not re-rate parcel mixes.
- The methodology bar IS the moat: most market "savings analysis" is benchmark comparison, not true shipment-level re-rating against the proposed rate card. The rigor Niklavs already operates at (engines, trust gate, bias-quarantine) is what the market mostly fakes.
- Gainshare fits this domain unusually well: invoice data gives auditable ground truth on both sides, nearly solving the attribution risk that makes gainshare fragile elsewhere.

SaaS productization parked for Phase 3 at earliest.
