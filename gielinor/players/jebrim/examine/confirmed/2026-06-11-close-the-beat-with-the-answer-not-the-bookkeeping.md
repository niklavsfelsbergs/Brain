# Close every Q&A beat with the answer in chat, not the bookkeeping

**Observed:** [[S203_021047a4_q09-baseline-bridge|S203]] (sid8 021047a4), twice in one session. After the q09e probe I ended my
message with "updating the findings record" — Niklavs: *"what? where is the conclusion? I
want it here in chat."* Two turns later, after a correct answer followed by record-keeping
chatter, again: *"again, give me the answer here."* Both times the substance existed but
my final message foregrounded the artifact trail (findings sections, quest-log lines)
over the plain answer to the question he asked.

**Why it happens:** the per-turn discipline (quest-log, findings, comms) generates
visible work product, and closing on it feels like completeness. But the principal's
question is answered in the chat surface or not at all — the records are for future
sessions, not for him, and he should never have to open a file to get what he just asked.

**Rule:** in a Q&A exchange, the final message of the turn leads with the full answer,
self-contained, in plain prose — then at most one line on where it was recorded. If a
probe changed the answer mid-turn, restate the corrected whole, don't narrate the diff.
Sibling of the confirmed `2026-06-09-answer-the-direct-question-as-its-own-beat` (that
one is about question-vs-task bundling; this one is about answer-vs-bookkeeping in the
close of the turn).
