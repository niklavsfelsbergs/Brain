# Durable-trace count is not the reality count — and use a work-signal, not a trigger-signal

**Observation ([[S215_12aa2f0f_rateproof-study-guide|S215]] amended close, 2026-06-12).** Niklavs asked how many sessions the EU tender has taken. I answered **66** (distinct tender quest files), then **~60** — both times he pushed back ("feels like way more," "it must be more, something we're not counting"). He was right both times. The real number was **~115 sessions that actually worked the tender**, out of **441 total Claude sessions** on the repo. My 66 only counted sessions that ran a *formal close ritual and left a tender quest file* — but most sessions `/clear` without closing, leaving no quest file, no SNNN, no comms entry. The durable traces under-counted reality by ~2×.

**Two distinct measurement errors I made:**

1. **Trace-count ≠ reality-count.** A count derived from artifacts that only exist on a *formal/successful path* (quest files, SNNN, CLOSINGs) systematically under-counts the informal path. The brain only writes those on a close; `/clear`-without-close is invisible to all of them. The ground truth was elsewhere — one `.claude/projects/*.jsonl` transcript per session (every `/clear` included).

2. **A mention-signal over-counts; use a work-signal.** My first "fuller" count (372 transcripts "mentioning" the tender) was nearly useless — the eu-tender digest *force-loads* on any carrier/tender cue, so a pure SCM session that said "carrier" once matched. The honest count came from a **work-signal**: depth of references to the actual repo path (1–2 = just the injected digest; 30+ = a heavy build/audit), plus tool calls that operated on tender files.

**How to apply.** When counting "how much / how many" from the system's own records: (a) ask what path the artifact is written on, and whether the thing you're counting also happens *off* that path (if so, the artifact under-counts — find the lower-level ground truth); (b) separate *triggered* from *worked* — a keyword/mention (especially one a hook force-injects) is not evidence of work; require a work-signal (a file op, an edit, depth of engagement). And when the principal's lived experience contradicts a clean number, **hunt for the uncounted rather than defend the number** — the convenient durable count is the suspect, not his memory.
