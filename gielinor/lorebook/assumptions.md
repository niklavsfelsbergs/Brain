## Format (for future entries)

Each assumption is a numbered item:

```
### A-001: <one-line claim>

**Stated.** YYYY-MM-DD. **Status.** active | challenged | invalidated.

**Claim.** <the assumption in one paragraph>

**Why we're assuming it.** <reason; what cheaper-than-confirming behavior it unlocks>

**What would invalidate it.** <the observation that would break it>

**Related.** <links to D-NNN decisions or other assumptions>
```

Assumptions are numbered globally and never renumbered. Invalidated assumptions stay in the file with `Status: invalidated` and a note pointing to the decision that retired them.
