## Progress

- Read research methodology.
- Broad scout 1: Duntes iela sits on the **boundary between Skanste and Pētersala-Andrejsala** apkaimes (Daugava right bank). Both are officially "Rīgas centrs"-adjacent compact-centre areas. Skanste = newest-developed district, ex-pasture/allotments → now modern offices, Arēna Rīga, new residential. Emergency medical centre at Duntes iela 8.
- Sources surfacing: skanste.lv, apkaimes.lv (Skanste + Pētersala-Andrejsala profiles), lv.wikipedia Duntes iela + Skanste, rdpad.lv resident surveys, merko/colliers dev pages.
- Project: "Duntes Ozoli", Duntes 28 — 16 floors, 217 units, op. since 2006/07, on a hill (upper-floor views), kindergarten on ground floor, underground/ground parking, 24h security. Listings file it variously under Skanste / centrs / **Sarkandaugava** (Latio) — telling that even agents disagree on the neighbourhood label.
- Duntes iela: 2204 m, 2 lanes, Northern District. Start section = Skanste/Brasa boundary; past the railway both sides = Sarkandaugava. Transport ON the street: buses 11, 24, 49 + trolleybus 3.
- Sky&More (Duntes 19A, across road): opened 2007, 13,000 m², **premium-positioned** (Max Mara, Armani brands), Rimi/Sky supermarket, pharmacy, fitness, restaurants, kids' centre. Pet-friendly.
- Skanste tram: planned 3.6 km low-floor line to Central Station (Rail Baltica hub). Still planning-stage.
- Skanste today: Xiaomi Arena, Rimi Olympic Centre/pool, office parks (Verde, Skanstes City), Skanstes Peaks towers, Hanzas Perons, Kim?. Projected 42,713 residents+workers by 2024 (vs 12,493 in 2017). 6.6 km bike route to Mežaparks ~25 min.
- KEY DOWNSIDE: council member Bergholcs — "pašvaldībai steidzami jāsāk risināt sabiedriskā transporta problēma Skanstē"; area develops faster than PT infrastructure. Most residents reach a stop in <5 min though. Ganību dambis magistrāle = noise complaints on parallel streets.
- Skanste transport detail: trams 5/9 (Ganību dambi), trolley 3 (Sarkandaugava→centre), buses 2/11/24/49. Rail tracks block westward link to Pētersala. Skanstes iela = main artery, reconstruction + tram planned.
- Transit to centre: trolley 3 → central market/Old Town fringe ~15-20 min (est, unverified). Postal code LV-1005 (Sarkandaugava/Pētersala zone, NOT central LV-1010/1050) confirms non-central siting.
