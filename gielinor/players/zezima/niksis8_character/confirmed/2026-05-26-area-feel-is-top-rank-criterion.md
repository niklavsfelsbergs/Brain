And the area was knowable from day one — Avoti, far-east Ģertrūdes, the "not prestige centrs" belt — it is literally *why the flat was cheaper*. - Distinguish this real area-no from the fault-finding reflex: when the objection is "how it feels to live/come home here," it's the genuine kind — honour it, don't try to argue it down with the discount.
