# `bank/drafts/` — about

Niklavs's half-formed design sketches. Things you're noodling on but haven't promoted to a decision, an assumption, or a research note.

**Distinct from the main brain's `drafts/`** — there, drafts are agent-generated content awaiting your approval. Here, drafts are *your own* unfinished thinking.

**Lifecycle.**
- Land here as a free-form file: `kebab-or-snake-case-name.md`.
- When the idea congeals: move to its proper home (a [[D-NNN]], a [[Q-NNN]], a research note) and delete the draft.
- When it's clearly abandoned: move to `bank/archive/drafts/` rather than delete — past sketches are sometimes the seed of later ideas.
