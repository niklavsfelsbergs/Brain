# Partial-alch a mid-quest player by draft-settledness, not by quest-status

**What changed.** The bankstanding Phase 0 rule (`spellbook/rituals/bankstanding.md` §0) gives three resolutions for a changed player: alch, skip, or — when the player has an in-progress quest — *flag-and-ask*, whose documented default is "skip the player for this round." This adds a fourth, sharper resolution to the flag-and-ask case: **partial-alch** — promote the harvest that is *settled and source-quest-independent*, hold the harvest that is *entangled with the still-open quest*.

**The discriminator.** A draft's promotability is governed by **the draft's own settledness, not by whether its source quest is closed.** A self-observation anchored to a correction that *already happened* (a bug caught and fixed, a reframe accepted) is settled the moment it's written — the open quest can keep running without changing whether the lesson is true. An *evolving artifact* — a build contract, a skill the open quest is still being built against, a bank note whose picture hasn't stabilized — is not settled; promoting it freezes a moving target.

**Anchor [[B-014_2026-06-01_fourteenth-bankstanding]] (2026-06-01, bankstanding).** S124 (shipping-report build) was live-in-progress and had produced four harvest items: three `examine/` self-observations (cross-check-two-derivations, verify-diffs-both-ways, don't-mechanize-judgment — each anchored to a bug already caught+fixed in the build) and one `spellbook/skills/` draft (`running-the-automated-shipping-report`, explicitly "the build contract," still being edited as the build completes its T-vs-T-1 test and triggering design). Flag-and-ask → principal chose partial-alch: the three settled self-obs promoted to confirmed, the evolving contract held until S124 closes.

**How to apply.** When flag-and-ask fires on a mid-quest player, don't default to whole-player skip. Sort the harvest: settled, build-independent identity/lesson drafts are promotable now; evolving contracts/skills/unstable bank notes wait for the quest to close. Recommend partial-alch when the split is clean. This complements [[feedback_bank_notes_harvest_from_finished_quests]] (don't draft *bank notes* alongside an active quest — the picture hasn't stabilized): bank notes are usually entangled (held); settled self-observations usually aren't (promotable). The settledness test is the unifying principle under both.
