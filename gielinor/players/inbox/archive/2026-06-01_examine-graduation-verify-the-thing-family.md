**The principle (proposed parent):** *Don't trust that a guarantee/measurement/capability works because the wiring looks right — exercise it from the real entry point and confirm it fires. Assumed limits and inferred state are the trap.*

## The constraint on consolidating

**Graduate, don't delete.** The value of each member is its specificity — each fires in a different situation (cached data / GUI claim / assumed limit / probe contamination). Collapsing them into one vague "always verify" rule reproduces exactly the anchorless, aspirational draft the drafts rule forbids. Correct shape = a **parent principle in global `examine/` that the specifics point up to** — a better-connected tree, not fewer rules. Per archive-discipline nothing is destroyed regardless.

## Why it's worth a graduation, not just the existing 3×-watch

The appears-3×-→-earn-a-skill reflex is per-player and skill-grained. This family is cross-player and examine-grained, and the global examine layer is the architecturally correct home for a transfer-level identity principle — which is precisely what's missing.
